Privacy Policy

DocFuse — PDF Tools & Scanner

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to DocFuse, developed by FusionLabs (Abdullah Al Shahed). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

DocFuse works completely offline. Your PDF files and scanned documents are never uploaded to any server and never leave your device.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal details
  • Your PDF files, images, or document content
  • Your real-time location data
  • Your contacts or device storage logs

What is collected by Third-Party Services:

DocFuse displays ads powered by Google AdMob and utilizes Google Play Services / ML Kit for document scanning. These services may collect certain device data to function properly.
  • Advertising ID (can be reset in your device settings)
  • Approximate location (country/region level only)
  • Device type, model, and OS version
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions) and basic diagnostics

How Your Files Are Handled

  • All PDF processing and conversion happens locally on your device.
  • Files are never uploaded to any server or shared with third parties.
  • We have no access to your documents, images, or text at any time.
  • Processed files are saved directly to your device storage.

App Permissions

  • Storage / Files: Required to read PDF files you select and save processed files to your device.
  • Camera: Required to scan documents and convert captured images into PDF or text.
  • Internet: Required only to load ads via Google AdMob. It is never used for document processing.

Third-Party Services

DocFuse uses Google AdMob for displaying ads and Google Play Services for ML Kit processing.

Children's Privacy

DocFuse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
